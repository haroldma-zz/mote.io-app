Where is mote.io?

A few months ago, mote.io, was bought by Zumicts. The app is under development for a wider and better remote for computers. Sadly, backwards compatibility with all previous releases is not capable. Until the new mote.io is ready, it will be pulled from all stores.

To stay up to date on development, follow on twitter @Zumicts

Mote.io is maintained by Zumicts.
